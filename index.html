About Us

The Game Changer

Bitcon Exchange token is a community focused multi-chain DeFi token aiming to bring transparency and anonymity into charity in the finance sector. Bitcon Exchange token has a maximum supply of 100 billion on the Ethereum blockchain and Binance Smart Chain. A 1% transaction tax is distributed among all holders, which means everyone that holds Bitcon Exchange token will receive a never ending cycle of passive income. Another 1% goes to the charity wallet for business grants.
